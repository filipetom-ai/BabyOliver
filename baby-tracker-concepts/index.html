Resumo rápido

Choose Calm Nest if...

You want the safest starting point: broad appeal, low stress, and an easy foundation for an MVP.

Choose Storybook if...

You want the app to feel personal and memory-driven, not just like a spreadsheet for parenting.

Choose Command Center if...

You care most about spotting patterns, coordinating caregivers, and making data useful.

Escolha Playroom se...

Você quer um produto com mais personalidade de marca, visual marcante e espaço para tendências úteis sem ficar com cara de planilha.

Choose Night Shift if...

Your main priority is speed and clarity during overnight feeds and sleepy one-handed use.
